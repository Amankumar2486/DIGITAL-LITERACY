# Responsible Social Media Checklist for College Students

### The Do's
* **Privacy Audit:** Regularly check your account privacy settings to control who sees your personal information .
* **Think Before Posting:** Ask yourself if you would be comfortable with a future employer or professor seeing the content .
* **Fact-Check:** Verify the authenticity of news or links before sharing them to prevent the spread of misinformation .
* **Network Professionally:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with alumni and industry professionals in the CSE field .
* **Respectful Engagement:** Maintain a polite and professional tone, even when you disagree with others online .

###  The Don'ts
* **Avoid Oversharing:** Do not post sensitive details like your home address, current precise location, or registration number .
* **No Cyberbullying:** Never use social media to harass, demean, or spread rumors about batchmates or faculty .
* **Don't Post Illegal Acts:** Avoid sharing photos or videos that depict policy violations or illegal activities .
* **Don't Plagiarize:** Never claim someone else’s digital art, code, or writing as your own without proper credit .
* **Ignore Suspicious Links:** Do not click on or share "clickbait" links that could lead to phishing or malware .
