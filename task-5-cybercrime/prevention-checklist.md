## Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)

* Use strong passwords that are hard to guess, and don’t use the same password everywhere.
* Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for your email, bank apps, and social media.
* Don’t click on random links or download files from unknown messages or emails.
* Avoid logging into important accounts on public Wi-Fi (like in cafes or hostels).
* Keep your phone and laptop updated so security bugs are fixed.
* Never share your OTP, password, or personal details with anyone, even if they sound official.
* Be careful with job offers, internships, or giveaways—they can be fake and target students.
* Download apps only from trusted places like Play Store or App Store.

### UPI / Financial Safety Tips:

* Always check the name and UPI ID before sending money.
* Don’t approve “collect requests” unless you are 100% sure.
* Remember: to receive money, you don’t need to scan a QR code or enter a PIN.
* Set a daily spending limit in your banking app for safety.

### Reporting Cybercrime:

* Report online at: **cybercrime.gov.in**
* Call **1930** for quick help in case of fraud
